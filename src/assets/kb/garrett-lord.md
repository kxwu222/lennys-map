## Summary
Garrett Lord, CEO of Handshake, reveals how he built a new business line on top of Handshake's existing college-to-career platform: the world's largest expert network for training frontier AI models. With zero customer acquisition costs and access to a massive audience of subject-matter experts (students and graduates), Handshake discovered that the only real moat in human data is access to the audience itself. The conversation explores unlimited AI demand, pivoting within an existing business, and seizing once-in-a-generation moments.

## Key Insight 1
As AI models improve, they no longer need generalist data labelers. What they need is domain experts for post-training refinement and evaluation. Handshake's strategic advantage is its existing network of millions of students and recent graduates who are genuine subject-matter experts across every field, providing expert-quality training data that directly advances model intelligence.

## Key Insight 2
Lord emphasizes the rarity of the current moment. He describes experiencing unlimited demand and urges founders to act with urgency and avoid regret. His advice: get on the plane to talk to a customer, make the late-night push, and check the data multiple times. Moments of unlimited demand in business are extraordinarily rare and must be seized aggressively.

## Key Insight 3
The tension between AI training and job displacement is addressed directly. Employers on Handshake are not reporting reduced hiring because of AI. Instead, AI is enabling humans to be more productive. The practical takeaway for platform companies is to look for adjacent AI-native business lines that leverage existing audiences and trust, rather than building from scratch.
